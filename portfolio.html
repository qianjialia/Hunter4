The Scenery of Yuyao

Yuyao is my hometown

My hometown Yuyao is not only a literary city with a history of 7000 years, but also a beautiful landscape city. In the early morning, the entire city slowly woke up with the rising sun. The trees on both sides of the Yao River are lush, and the sunlight shines on the river surface, shimmering with waves. Occasionally, ships pass by, creating layers of ripples. People who wake up early may form groups of three or five, chatting and laughing; Some people exercise alone, either jogging or briskly walking by the Yao River, creating a beautiful scenery. Standing on the newly built bridge, looking east, is the ancient Tongji Bridge. It has a history of hundreds of years and has witnessed the development of Yuyao. On the north bank of the bridge, there is a row of quaint and elegant old-fashioned buildings with bars inside Tea houses, antique shops, etc. Here, fashion and simplicity are perfectly integrated. At night, the lights here are shining brightly, creating a lively scene. Looking west, there is a famous mountain in Yuyao - Longquan Mountain. Climbing up the stairs, the mountain is filled with birds chirping and fragrant flowers, and the trees are lush and evergreen all year round, as if putting a green coat on Longquan Mountain. As a small oxygen bar in the city, Longquan Mountain has attracted countless citizens to come and climb, making it a great place for leisure. At night, the mountains are shimmering with colorful lights, green, orange, and yellow, shining together like a painting, leaving one lingering and unforgettable.
